Team Rules

Communication: Organizational: Text group chat
Project: Slack
Client: Slack/email
If a team member must miss a meeting: Team meeting (Mon 4:30PM): Notify text group chat at least 1 hour in advance
Mentor meeting (Fri 1:30PM): Notify text group chat at least 1 day in advance
Client meeting (Mon 4:00PM): Notify text group chat at least 1 day in advance Monday 4 PM
Team behavior: Team members must try to answer team questions within the same day, within reason.
Technical questions should be prioritized.
Client emails and messages must be discussed and responded to within 24 hours.
Team members must take individual notes at every meeting.
Coding behavior: Comment code as appropriate and adhere to style guidelines
Create variables that describe their function and which follow a consistent theme.
Pull code before pushing updates.
Use individual branches for development and only merge code into master once the code has been tested in the dev branch first and is confirmed to be functional.
Keep the Trello board up-to-date by assigning yourself to tasks you are working on, and moving your cards to the appropriate list for each stage of development.
